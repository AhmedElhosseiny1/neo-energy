## Service 1: On-Grid Solar System Installation

Neo Energy provides grid-connected solar system installation for properties that remain linked to the national electricity grid.

### Ideal Use Cases

- Residential: reduce electricity bills and adopt renewable energy.
- Commercial: lower operational energy costs.
- Industrial: improve efficiency and reliability using solar power.

### Benefits

- Lower electricity costs with net metering.
- Efficient generation through high-performance solar panels.
- Integration with existing grid infrastructure.

## Service 2: Solar Pumping Systems for Agriculture

Solar pumping systems are presented as a solution for farmers and agribusinesses seeking stable water access without dependence on diesel generators.

### Benefits

- Reliable and sustainable water supply.
- Reduced fuel dependency and generator costs.
- Lower-emission agricultural operations.

## Service 3: Power Purchase Agreement (PPA)

Neo Energy offers a PPA model where the client hosts a solar energy system without paying the upfront system cost.

### How PPA Works

| Step / Concept | Explanation |
|---|---|
| No upfront system cost | Neo Energy installs and operates the solar system on the client’s premises. |
| Pay for electricity used | The client pays for generated electricity, usually below traditional utility rates. |
| Long-term savings | Energy cost reduction and more predictable electricity rates. |
| Maintenance-free | Neo Energy is responsible for operations, monitoring, and maintenance. |

### PPA Benefits

- No need for technical expertise or major capital expenditure.
- Lower energy bills.
- Sustainability and reduced carbon footprint.
- Flexible contract durations from 10 to 25 years.
