# Product — Elsewedy H1Z2Z2-K DC Solar Cable

**Source URL:** https://www.neo-es.com/shop/cables/elsewedy-h1z2z2-k

## Product Summary

Elsewedy H1Z2Z2-K is positioned as a reliable single-core flexible DC solar cable for photovoltaic systems. Neo Energy presents it as suitable for residential rooftops, commercial plants, and industrial solar installations.

## Key Features

| Feature | Detail |
|---|---|
| Voltage rating | Up to 1.8 kV DC to earth |
| Durability | 25+ years expected lifetime |
| Temperature resistance | -40°C to +90°C operating range; conductor temperatures up to 120°C; short-circuit tolerance up to 250°C for 5 seconds |
| Fire safety | Halogen-free, low-smoke, flame-retardant; compliant with EN 50618 and IEC 62930 |
| Flexibility and strength | Flexible tinned copper conductor, Class 5 |
| Weather resistance | UV and outdoor-weather resistant |
| Sizes | 1.5 mm² to 16 mm² |
| Colors | Red, blue, black |
